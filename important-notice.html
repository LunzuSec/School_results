Important Announcement

Published: January 3, 2026

School Opening for Term 2 - Academic Year 2025/2026

School opens for term 2 on 5th January, 2026. All students are expected to report on 4th January, 2026 with the following requirements:

  • Proof of Payment: School fees payment receipt or bank deposit slip
  • Required Materials:
    • Mosquito nets (for boarding students)
    • Pails (2 per student)
    • Tissue papers (2 packets)
    • Soap and other personal toiletries
  • Academic Requirements:
    • All textbooks and exercise books
    • Mathematical sets and geometry instruments
    • School uniform in good condition
    • Sports attire
  • Health Requirements:
    • Medical report for students with special conditions
    • Personal medication with doctor's prescription
Important Dates
  • Reporting Day: January 4, 2026 (8:00 AM - 4:00 PM)
  • Classes Begin: January 5, 2026 (7:30 AM)
  • Fee part-payment Deadline: January 5, 2026
  • First Term Exams: March 16-April, 1, 2026
