Published: January 3, 2026

School Opening for Term 3 - Academic Year 2025/2026

School opens for term 3 on 20th April, 2026. All students are expected to report on 19th April, 2026 with the following requirements:

  • Proof of Payment: School fees payment receipt or bank deposit slip
  • Required Materials:
    • • Mosquito nets (for boarding students)
    • • Pails (2 per student)
    • • Tissue papers (2 packets)
    • • Soap and other personal toiletries
  • Academic Requirements:
    • • All textbooks and exercise books
    • • Mathematical sets and geometry instruments
    • • School uniform in good condition
  • Health Requirements:
    • • Medical report for students with special conditions
    • • COVID-19 vaccination card (where applicable)
Important Dates
  • Reporting Day: April 19, 2026 (8:00 AM - 4:00 PM)
  • Classes Begin: April 20, 2026 (7:30 AM)
  • Fee part-payment Deadline: April 19, 2026
  • First Term Exams: March 16 - April 1, 2026
