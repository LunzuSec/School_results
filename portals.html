How to View Your Results

Before You Proceed:
  • Requirements: You'll need your student name, class, and date of birth
  • Browser: Use Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari for best experience
  • Support: Contact technical support if you encounter any issues
  • Timing: Results are available 24/7 during the results period
Step-by-Step Guide:
  1. Click the "Go to Results Portal" button in the student portal
  2. Select your class from the dropdown menu
  3. Wait for student names to load (this may take a few seconds)
  4. Select your name from the list or search for it
  5. Enter your date of birth in YYYY-MM-DD format
  6. Click "View Results" to see your results in a new tab
  7. Optional: Click "Download PDF" to save a copy of your results
Troubleshooting Tips
  • If the page doesn't load, try refreshing or using a different browser
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Check that you're entering your details correctly
  • If issues persist, contact the technical support team
  • If you can't find your name, please ensure you've selected the correct class
