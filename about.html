About Our School

A Legacy of Excellence Since 1985

Lunzu Secondary School is a distinguished government-run institution serving both boys and girls in Blantyre Rural. Established in 1985, our school has built a strong reputation for academic excellence and holistic development.

Academic Excellence
Consistent top performance
Inclusive Education
Co-educational environment

We take pride in preparing students for higher education and professional careers, with numerous alumni successfully transitioning to colleges and universities nationwide.

The school maintains high educational standards while fostering a supportive learning environment for all students, helping them achieve their full potential both academically and personally.

Established 1985

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values to become responsible citizens and leaders in society.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in secondary education, producing well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to national development.

School History

1985

Lunzu Secondary School was established as a government school serving the Lunzu community.

1995

First batch of students sat for Malawi School Certificate of Education (MSCE) examinations.

2005

School celebrated 20 years of existence with major infrastructure development.

2024

A student from Lunzu Secondary, Martin Master, achieved position 1 in the National Mathematics Olympiad organized by Old Mutual, and the school celebrated this success.

2025

Launch of Digital Results Management System to modernize academic tracking.
