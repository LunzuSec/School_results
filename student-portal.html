How to Use the Results Portal
  1. Click the "Go to Results Portal" button above
  2. Select your class from the dropdown menu
  3. Wait for student names to load (this may take a few seconds)
  4. Select your name from the list or search for it
  5. Enter your date of birth in YYYY-MM-DD format
  6. Click "View Results" to see your results in a new tab
  7. Optional: Click "Download PDF" to save a copy of your results
If you can't find your name, please ensure you've selected the correct class.
